# GlassGPT — Domain Glossary

## GlassGPT

The iOS companion app that extends the Meta AI glasses experience on Apple devices.

## Registration

The process by which GlassGPT is approved as a permitted integration inside the Meta AI app (under Developer Mode). Registration is distinct from device permissions such as camera access.

## Session owner

The Apple device that currently runs the active GlassGPT session (conversation, agent, or UI). Reserved for future iPhone ↔ Mac handoff; not in scope for the registration shell.

## Live stream

Real-time video from the Meta Ray-Ban glasses camera, delivered to GlassGPT through the DAT camera stream. Distinct from photos and videos stored on the glasses and synced later by Meta AI.
